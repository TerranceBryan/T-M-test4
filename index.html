Genesis of T & M

One night after thoughts of how hard it had become being jobless and not even being able to secure a position as an intern a year after college an idea spark Thomas' mind.Why not create job opportunity for himself and even other friends going through the same hardship. T for Thomas his first name and M for Macarious his late grandpa.Not only did the name fit perfectly but also demonstrated his aim of moving into new ages and technological world of 'Thomas' from the usual technological or analog world of 'Macarious'. And so, the story continues—an evolving narrative of passion, perseverance, and the unwavering belief that, through technology, we can shape a better future for businesses and communities alike. The journey is ongoing, the challenges are ever-changing, but the vision remains clear—to be at the forefront of innovation, driving positive change in the world of technology.
